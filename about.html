About RRPANA

Learn about our mission, vision, and the people behind our social initiatives

Our Story

RRPANA was founded with a vision to create meaningful social impact through targeted activities that address pressing community needs. Our organization is built on the principle that every individual deserves opportunities for growth and every community deserves support in its development journey.

Under the leadership of Shri Manoj Vithhalrao Dhawale, RRPANA has grown from a small community initiative to an organization that implements diverse social programs across multiple areas of community welfare.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual has access to opportunities for growth, education, and wellbeing, and where communities are empowered to create sustainable positive change.

We envision a future where social welfare initiatives are not just temporary fixes but catalysts for long-term community transformation.
